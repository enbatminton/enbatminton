En-Badminton is

We are a group that operates in Tokyo (Shinjuku, Itabashi, Nerima) to enjoy badminton, and we are looking for members who are lacking in exercise or who want to try badminton.

The main activity involves playing games, with beginners and experienced players mixed together or divided into levels.

Features of the circle

The age range is wide, from 20s to 40s.

The levels vary from beginner to experienced, but there are more beginners.

The male to female ratio is about 6:4, with more men than women in most cases.

How to Join

Flow of the day

Step1:Confirm participation registration

BAND(URL:https://band.us/n/a5adAcA8K5Aak) Check that you have registered the dates that you would like to attend and that are convenient for you

Step2:To the gym

Go to the date, time and location (gymnasium) listed on the schedule.

Step3: Change clothes

First, change in the locker room. When you're ready, head to the gym.

Step4: Warm-up

Everyone does warm-up exercises and practice to avoid injury.

Step5: Doubles Games

Start of the game At first, a mix of beginners and experienced players. After that, the games are divided by level.
Matches are divided using the application.

Step6: Cleanup and dismissal

We will give the dismissal order well in advance before leaving the building completely. We also ask for your cooperation in cleaning up.

date

The following dates are scheduled. Please register on the application so that we know how many people will attend. Please note that we may have to cancel the event due to the gymnasium.
